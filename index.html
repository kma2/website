Design Work

Mark43 RMS

Product Design (Current Role)¬∑ 2018 - Present

The RMS is where police officers write reports, detectives manage their case loads, evidence clerks track items, and records administrators handle all the behind-the-scenes workflows. Some things I deal with in my daily work include: legal reporting requirements, preventing potential for abuse, information density, accessibility, quality of user-entered data, handling sensitive information, and interactions with other government systems. Our users work in many different functional roles, have a range of attitudes towards technology, and are often in high-stress situations where they must act responsibly while moving quickly and staying safe.

I'm lucky to work with passionate and brilliant colleagues. The work challenges, motivates, and humbles me every day.
