Katy Ma

I'm a designer based in Brooklyn, New York who embraces complex systems, processes, and problem spaces. Currently, I am a Product Design Lead at Valon, creating a transparent, fair, and worry-free mortgage servicing experience. Previously, I worked on records management tools for first responders at Mark43 and earned a degree in Media Arts & Sciences from Wellesley College.

I've also been lucky to work and learn with wonderful people at these places: Google (YouTube Live & Gaming), MIT Media Lab (Civic Media), MIT Design Lab, Microsoft (Garage), Kensho, and State Street (CAR).

Outside of digital design, I love making things with my hands using fiber, clay, paint, and simple tools. In my free time, you'll find me training my puppy Nuomi and engaging in mutual aid efforts in my neighborhood.

Mark43 RMS

Product Designer ¬∑ 2018 - 2020

The Records Management System is where officers write reports, detectives manage their case loads, evidence clerks track items, and records administrators handle all the behind-the-scenes workflows. Some design challenges this work entailed: legal reporting requirements, preventing potential for abuse, information density, accessibility, quality of user-entered data, handling sensitive information, and interactions with other government systems. Users work in many different functional roles, have a range of attitudes towards technology, and are often in high-stress situations where they must act responsibly while moving quickly and staying safe.
